About Me

Driving AI Excellence with Purpose and Precision

I am Prajwal Arjun Kanade, a dedicated developer specializing in machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and backend development. With a solid understanding of AI concepts and hands-on experience applying these skills to real-world challenges, I have cultivated a proficiency that enables me to design robust and scalable solutions. My journey through AI and Data Science has equipped me with the technical skills and strategic thinking necessary to develop innovative, effective AI-driven applications.

Throughout my career, I have worked on impactful projects such as age and gender detection in live CCTV streams and vehicle detection systems using YOLOv5 and YOLOv8 models. These experiences involved designing complex detection algorithms, optimizing model performance, and implementing real-time tracking solutions. My proficiency with YOLO models has enabled me to achieve high accuracy rates in object detection, while my work with DeepFace and RetinaFace for face analysis showcases my ability to solve challenging problems in real-time.

In addition to computer vision, I have a strong interest in natural language processing (NLP) and large language models (LLMs). I am particularly focused on developing AI-powered tutoring systems that leverage deep learning for both structured and unstructured data analysis. My hands-on experience with Python and libraries such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, and OpenCV allows me to create efficient machine learning pipelines. I also have expertise in optimizing model inference using CUDA and TensorRT, ensuring high performance in real-time applications.

Furthermore, I possess skills in backend development using Django and Django REST Framework (DRF) to create RESTful APIs for applications such as leave management and payroll systems. This experience has deepened my understanding of scalable API design and secure data management. With a commitment to continuous learning, I am eager to advance my skills and integrate emerging technologies into my projects. Whether deploying models on edge devices like Jetson Nano or exploring advanced NLP concepts, I am passionate about leveraging my expertise to solve real-world problems and push the boundaries of AI technology.
